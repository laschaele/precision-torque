How to Prepare for a DMV Visit

DMV visits take time — being prepared reduces delays. Here’s a quick checklist to help you avoid extra trips.

Bring correct ID

Driver license or state-issued ID for the owner listed on title/registration.

Bring ownership documents

Title (signed if transferring) or registration renewal notice and any supporting forms required by your county.

Proof of insurance

Active coverage documentation that meets state minimums.

Payment and forms

Check accepted payment methods for your county and complete forms ahead of time if possible.

Tip: Check your county DMV website for appointment availability and online services before going in person.
