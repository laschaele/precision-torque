Preparing for Vehicle Registration & Title Transfer

Whether you're renewing registration or transferring title, having the right documents ready can save you time at the DMV. Below is a practical checklist tailored for Texas processes — confirm with your local DMV for county-specific rules.

Essential documents

  • Valid photo ID for the owner(s)
  • Proof of vehicle ownership (current title or manufacturer's certificate of origin)
  • Completed application forms (varies by county — check local DMV site)
  • Proof of insurance (minimum required for registration)

If transferring title

  • Signed title from seller — signatures must be in the correct places
  • Odometer disclosure (for certain vehicle ages)
  • Bill of sale (recommended) with sale date and price

Fees and payment

Bring payment for registration and title fees. Many counties accept cards, cash, or check — check your county's payment options ahead of time.

Quick tip: If you're short on time, check whether the county offers online services for certain renewals — some transactions can be completed without an in-person DMV visit.
